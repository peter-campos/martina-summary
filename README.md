## 📌 About

**El Viaje de Martina** is an educational mobile app developed in collaboration with **Universidad de los Andes** (Colombia) and the **University of Southampton** (UK), designed to inform migrant women about their sexual and reproductive rights in an accessible, offline-first format.

The app was published on the **App Store (May 20, 2024)** and **Google Play (May 11, 2024)**. The project agreement with the client has since ended and the app is no longer available in the stores. With Coolearning Consulting LLC's permission, the app is distributed directly for demonstration purposes.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📖 Informational content on sexual and reproductive rights for migrant women
- 🎙️ Embedded podcast player with offline support
- 📵 Fully offline — no internet connection required after installation
- 🌐 Available in Spanish
